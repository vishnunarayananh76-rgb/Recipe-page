Creamy Garlic Pasta

A delicious homemade pasta recipe ready in just 30 minutes.

⏱ Time

30 Minutes

🍽 Servings

4 People

⭐ Difficulty

Easy

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Melt butter and sauté chopped garlic for 2 minutes.
  3. Add fresh cream and simmer gently.
  4. Mix in grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Add cooked pasta and stir well.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and parsley.
  7. Serve hot with extra cheese on top.
